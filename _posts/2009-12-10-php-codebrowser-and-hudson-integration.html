How can add this reports to Hudson ? Use HTML Publisher Plugin! It's easy, add to your build script new target phpcb in this style: <target name="phpcb"> <exec executable="phpcb" dir="${basedir}/source"> <arg line="--log reports/logs/ --source source/ --output reports/phpcb/" /> </exec> </target> Don't forget install HTML Publisher Plugin and make in job settings to directory with reports: Reports are done:
